InvisiMaze

Right now the canvas has a fixed size and no collision check, I'll be making it dynamic and I will be adding collision in the future!

For the full experience you need a keyboard and mouse
Use WASD to move.

If you are on a smartphone/tablet, click on debug and press anywhere on the canvas. Due to the size of the canvas, the mobile version still a bit bugged.
