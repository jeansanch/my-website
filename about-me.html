About Me

How did all started?

It all started with me wanting to automate every game that I've played. So I've found out about macros. The first real "coding" with macros that I've made, was using a mod for Minecraft.

What am I most proud of?

Reverse engineering a mobile game from one of the major companies in the game industry. I was able to manipulate spawn positions, sprites, images and even managed to play on unfinished stages of the game.

Plans for the future?

I'm still unsure with what I really want to work, but I have a big passion for AI, Machine Learning, testing and security.
(What if, I make an AI to try to find security flaws?!)

Other things that I would like to tell?

I like a good CTF, I love to understand (or try to understand) how things work, I mean, how some glitches happens, like the MissingNo. and Super Mario World Credits Warp. Those things are amazing!
